3-Minute Priming Exercises

Use 3 minutes to read the case notes, plan your structure, and get into the examiner's mindset.

How to use this:

  • Click New Priming Case to generate a patient scenario with case notes.
  • Start the 3-minute timer and use this time to read the notes, identify key issues, plan your consultation structure, and think about what the examiner might be looking for.
  • After the timer ends (or anytime), click View Model Answer to see the priming focus and examiner's lens - this shows what you should have considered and what the examiner wants to see.
