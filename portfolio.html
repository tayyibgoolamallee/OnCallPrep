The Three-Link Rule: Your Secret Weapon

Every reflection should link to three competencies. This isn't just a rule‚Äîit's your path to writing reflections that actually demonstrate growth:

  • Holistic understanding - Show you see the whole patient, not just the problem
  • Integration of skills - Demonstrate how different competencies work together
  • Higher-level reflection - Move beyond "what happened" to "what I learned and how I'll apply it"

High-Yield Reflections: Work Smarter, Not Harder

Strategic Focus by Stage: Your Roadmap

Each stage has different priorities. Here's how to approach them strategically:

1

ST1: Foundation

This is about building confidence and understanding the system. Don't rush. Quality over quantity‚Äîevery reflection should teach you something. Focus on getting comfortable with the process.

2

ST2: Development
