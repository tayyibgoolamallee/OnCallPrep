Portfolio Strategy

The Three-Link Rule

Every reflection should link to three competencies. This demonstrates:

  • Holistic understanding of patient care
  • Integration of multiple skills
  • Reflective practice at a higher level

High-Yield Reflections

Focus your reflections on cases that demonstrate:

  • Complex decision-making - Multiple differentials, uncertainty
  • Multi-system involvement - Cases spanning different competencies
  • Learning moments - Mistakes, near-misses, new learning
  • Professional development - Leadership, teamwork, communication

Strategic Focus by Stage

1

ST1: Foundation

Focus on basic competencies, building confidence, understanding systems. Quality over quantity - ensure each reflection is meaningful.

2

ST2: Development

Increase complexity, demonstrate independent decision-making. Start AKT preparation. Show progression from ST1.

3

ST3: Exit

Demonstrate readiness for independent practice. Focus on complex cases, leadership, and areas for ongoing development.
