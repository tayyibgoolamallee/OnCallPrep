ST1 Guide

Building Your Portfolio Base

ST1 is your foundation year. Focus on understanding the system, building confidence, and establishing good portfolio habits. Quality over quantity - ensure each reflection demonstrates genuine learning.

ST1 WPBA Requirements

Minimum requirements per 6-month placement:

  • ‚úìCase-Based Discussions (CBDs): Minimum 4 (at least 2 from hospital, 2 from GP if applicable)
  • ‚úìConsultation Observation Tools (COTs): Minimum 2 if in GP placement
  • ‚úìMulti-Source Feedback (MSF): 1 per year (minimum 8 responses)
  • ‚úìPatient Satisfaction Questionnaire (PSQ): 1 if in GP placement
  • ‚úìReflective Entries: Minimum 4 per 6-month placement
  • ‚úìLearning Log: Regular entries documenting learning activities
  • ‚úìPersonal Development Plan (PDP): Created and reviewed with Educational Supervisor
  • ‚úìClinical Supervisor Report (CSR): From each placement

Strategic Focus Areas

1. Building Foundation Knowledge

  • Understand common presentations in primary care
  • Learn consultation models and frameworks
  • Develop history-taking and examination skills
  • Understand referral pathways and local services

2. Portfolio Quality

  • Start early - don't leave everything to the last minute
  • Write reflections soon after events while details are fresh
  • Link to multiple competencies (aim for 3 links per reflection)
  • Show progression and learning, not just what happened

3. Professional Development

  • Build relationships with supervisors and colleagues
  • Attend teaching sessions and workshops
  • Engage with your Educational Supervisor regularly
  • Document learning from all sources (clinical, courses, reading)
